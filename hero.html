Bugle Hero

The game screen appears here if your browser supports the Canvas API.

Press the indicated key and SPACE BAR at the SAME TIME in time with the counter to play the note.

Use the arrow keys, PAGE UP, or PAGE DOWN to scroll through the different calls.

The number of "*" indicates the difficulty of the call. The more "*" the more difficult the call is to play.
